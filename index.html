A VERTICALLY INTEGRATED REAL ESTATE DEVELOPMENT AND MANAGEMENT PLATFORM

Alma Companies creates equity value along every stage of the deal cycle through multiple operating verticals: an acquisitions team specializing in off-market deal sourcing; a fully integrated construction company; a warehouse and operations logistics team, and a tech-first people and property management platform.

SERVING HOMEOWNERS THE FASTEST AND HIGHEST SALE OR FINANCING OFFERS, AND MAKING LANDLORDING EASY.

Alma Home Marketplace serves the multi-trillion-dollar market of landlords and homeowners wanting to quickly sell or avoid losing their home, through sale leaseback options, fractional equity sales, or guaranteed above-market rental income in exchange for a five-year purchase option.

Home Marketplace also offers cash advances to source and manage construction through Alma Companies to improve rental income or sales price.
